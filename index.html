Country Cocktail

Many have asked what we mean by "country cocktail".

For ladies, it's probably a bit easier to define: sun dresses, floral or summery prints, pastels, etc.

For gents, it's easier to define by what not to wear: no tuxes, no black suits (if possible), ties optional.

Basically we want you to have fun with the dress code; dress stylishly yet comfortably. Greys and blues will look great. Try checks or plaids. Rock a pair of loafers. Bonus points from the bride for anyone who can pull off a bolo tie and/or cowboy boots.
